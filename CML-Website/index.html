About CML-GNDEC

Center for the Multi-Faceted Learning is a hub for imparting ethical and general purpose knowledge in terms of education, morality and humility. The concept of this program is to utilize all the capabilities of the students to negotiate and clinch a top quality communication skills, problem solving skills and develop distinctive personality. This absolutely helps students to identify their fields of interest . A lot of activities under this updated society surely help the students to explore and have broader view of their selective fields. In addition to this center also encourage students for enrolling into MOOCs and get involved in self learning which will lead to a incremental knowledge and development of interest in different fields. Being a part of this club an individual can primarily build not only his/her personal individualization but also enrich their mind-set with optimistic thoughts, constructive behaviour and confidence.
